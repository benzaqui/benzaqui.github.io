Privacy policy

Ma Voix · iPhone app

Last updated: 2026-04-12

Who we are

Ma Voix is published as a mobile application on Apple’s App Store / TestFlight. The data controller for personal data processed in connection with the app is the individual or organisation named as the seller in App Store Connect (see the app listing). For questions about this policy, use the contact email shown on the App Store page for Ma Voix.

What data the app processes

On your iPhone (local storage)
  • Phrases you type, play, or save as favorites.
  • Light usage patterns used to improve suggestions and optional “people” hints.
  • App preferences needed for the app to function.
Sent to third-party services (only when you use those features)
  • Cloud text-to-speech: the text (and technical voice identifiers) may be sent to the provider configured in the app (e.g. ElevenLabs) over HTTPS to generate audio.
  • Cloud suggestions: recent text context may be sent over HTTPS to the provider configured in the app (e.g. Anthropic) to propose short phrases. You are not required to use this feature.

We do not sell your personal data. There is no in-app advertising and no Ma Voix “account” login in the app itself.

Legal bases (summary)

Processing is necessary to provide the communication features you ask for when you tap “speak” or use suggestions, and to remember favorites and on-device learning you enable. Where cloud providers are involved, they act as processors under their terms and privacy notices.

Retention

Data kept on the device remains until you delete the app or clear in-app storage where the app offers that option. Provider-side retention depends on the third party’s policies for requests they receive when you use cloud features.

Children

The app is intended for adults in a family / care context. If you believe a child’s data was processed inappropriately, contact us via the App Store listing contact information.

Changes

We may update this page when the app or its data practices change. The “Last updated” date at the top will change accordingly. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy where required by law.

Français (résumé)

Ma Voix stocke sur l’iPhone les phrases, favoris et apprentissages locaux nécessaires au fonctionnement. Les fonctions vocales ou suggestions « cloud » envoient le texte utile à ce moment-là aux prestataires concernés (connexion HTTPS).
